## 🌍 **Susu–Koré Sèbèli Language Translator & Learning App**
A modern, AI‑powered platform designed to preserve, teach, and celebrate the Susu language and the Koré Sèbèli writing system. Whether you're a beginner, a heritage speaker, or a researcher, this app gives you the tools to learn, translate, and practice Susu with clarity and confidence.

---

### ⭐ **What the App Does**
- **Instant Translation**  
  Translate between **Susu**, **Koré Sèbèli**, and **English** with high accuracy. Perfect for daily communication, study, and cultural preservation.

- **Koré Sèbèli Script Support**  
  Learn the full writing system with stroke‑by‑stroke guidance, character charts, and AI‑generated examples.

- **Smart Dictionary**  
  A growing, structured dictionary with:
  - Part‑of‑speech tagging  
  - Example sentences  
  - Multiple voice pronunciations (male, female, child)  
  - Audio playback for every entry  
  - Usage notes and cultural context  

- **Phrasebook for Real Conversations**  
  Categories include greetings, travel, emergencies, daily life, and more — each with audio and Koré Sèbèli script.

- **AI Learning Assistant**  
  Practice speaking, reading, and writing with an interactive tutor that adapts to your level.

- **Offline Mode**  
  Access essential vocabulary and phrases even without internet.

### 🌱 **Why It Matters**
Susu is a vibrant language with deep cultural roots. Koré Sèbèli is a modern script designed to give Susu a strong written identity. This app brings both together — preserving heritage while empowering the next generation of learners and creators.
